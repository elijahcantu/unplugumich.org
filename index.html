Unplug UMich is a student organization focused on helping individuals manage their screen time. We offer meetings to assist with device restrictions, content blocking, and personalized strategies for healthier digital habits. Our goal is to create a supportive space where members can connect with accountability partners to reduce screen time and improve well-being.
